Here's What LC Has Been Up To Since 'Love Is Blind'

August 2024 · 5 minute read

Many stars of the Netflix reality series Love Is Blind have become super famous. Fans are curious about Jessica Batten's engagement and also can't get enough of the juicy story of her relationship with Mark Cuevas on the show. There's another cast member who fans love and that's Lauren Chamblin. The reality star, who likes going by "LC," was also interested in starting a relationship with Matt Barnett and it was sad to see him leave her behind and choose Amber Pike. While many fans think that Barnett and Amber are adorable together, LC hasn't been lucky in love and she seems so sweet that it's a real shame.

RELATED: 'Love Is Blind': What Has Mark Cuevas Been Up To Since The Show Ended?

Season 1 of Love Is Blind began streaming on Netflix in February 2020 and according to Pop Sugar, the episodes were filmed in 2018. It's been a long time since LC appeared on the series, so what has she been up to? Keep reading to find out everything that LC has done since starring on Love Is Blind.

6 LC Became An Account Manager

When looking at the net worths of the Love Is Blind cast, no one is sure how much money LC has in the bank, but she has a great career, and she has been working hard since she filmed season 1 of the show.

According to LC's Linked In Page, she is an Account Manager for a company called Premiere Onboard. Reality Tidbit reported that LC worked at VocoVision before that and was a recruiter. LC also has experience as a Marketing Representative and held that job in 2017 and 2018.

5 LC Started A Podcast

Fans of Love Is Blind will be thrilled to know that LC started a podcast recently. She and her friend Tiffany Danielle host a podcast called "Permission To Enter" and episodes are released on Thursdays.

RELATED: 'Love Is Blind': Were The Weddings Real Or Fake?

The podcast is all about love, dating, and relationships so it's relatable for anyone who is single or who wants to hear two friends talk about these important subjects. LC and Tiffany also have some cool people on the podcast and they interviewed psychic Professor Jesse King, a registered dietitian named Megan, and Erin Clare Jones, a human design expert. Many Love Is Blind fans appreciate that LC is honest about being single and wanting to meet a partner, so it's nice to have another format to hear from her.

4 LC Became A Social Media Influencer

Many reality stars enjoy promoting products on Instagram and since starring on Love Is Blind, it looks like LC has also become a social media influencer.

LC does a great job talking about the products that she enjoys using. LC has partnered with Kroger and in a recent video, she shared that she made a White Bean and Kale soup. LC also shared a video of a visit that she made to Baker Farms to talk about farm to table food.

3 LC Made Good Friends From Starring On 'Love Is Blind'

A reality show like Love Is Blind is an interesting case. On the one hand, the cast members spent a lot of time together when they weren't talking to potential partners in the pods, and they got a chance to get to know each other and connect. On the other hand, they were competing for proposals and in the case of LC, she liked Matt Barnett at the same time that Jessica Batten and Amber Pike did.

LC made friends from Love Is Blind, however, and it seems like these relationships flourished after the show. LC said in an interview with Sarah Scoop, “…I’m probably the closest with Diamond…Lexi is one of my really good friends…there’s a lot of people I am still really good friends with…”

2 LC Appeared On 'Love Is Blind: After The Altar'

Netflix allowed fans to see their favorite Love Is Blind cast members again in the three part reunion series After The Altar. LC talked about dating Mark and how he cheated on her, and it was a shame to hear.

RELATED: Does Jessica Batten Regret Starring On ‘Love Is Blind’?

While fans didn't learn a lot about LC's life since the show, it was nice to see LC in a good, happy place as she seems to be continuing to search for love while keeping a positive attitude.

1 LC Encouraged People To Be Politically Active

It's always inspiring when celebrities use their social media platforms for a good cause and in December 2020, LC posted on Instagram and said that people living in Georgia should vote in the election.

LC has shown in other social media posts that she is politically active. In July 2020, she talked about the femicide in Turkey and wrote in the caption that people should care about the world around them: "One of the best things we can do to show support for our fellow women right now is to VOTE. I challenge you to register if you’re not and to ask at least 2 others if they are registered! I also challenge you to wear a mask, to sign a petition about a cause you care about, to keep protesting because BLM, to call your senator, to buy full price from your friends businesses..."
