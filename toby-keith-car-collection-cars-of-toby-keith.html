Overview

Toby Keith Covel is an American singer, songwriter, and actor. He started his career in 1993 with his first album ‘Toby Keith,’ and has been a great singer and huge celebrity. Keith’s first single ‘Should’ve Been a Cowboy’ was a big hit and was at number one on the U.S. Billboard Hot Country Songs chart in 1993. Apart from acting he is also into acting and made his acting debut with Broken Bridges in 2006. Keith has an expert-level taste in fine automobiles and made a huge collection of them over the years. Let’s have a look at the Toby Keith car collection.

1. 1963 Chevy Impala

This rear-wheel drive vehicle has a 3.8L 6-cylinder engine that generates a maximum power output of 140hp at 4400 rpm. This can accelerate from 0-60 mph in 13.4 sec and is priced at around $3,196..

2. Mercedes-Benz GLS-450

The Mercedes is powered by a 3.0L turbocharged inline-six engine and is assisted by a 48-volt hybrid system that is capable of generating a maximum power output of 362 hp. This SUV can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 5.5 seconds. It costs $78,250.

3. Ford F150 Harley Davidson

This truck is a limited Harley Davidson edition model that Ford made to match with the motorcycles of Harley Davidson owners. This has a supercharged 5.4-liter fuel-injected V8 engine that produces a maximum of 260 horsepower and can accelerate from 0-60mph in 6.2 seconds.

4. Ford F150 Raptor

This vehicle is equipped with a 3.5L V6 EcoBoost engine that is capable of producing a maximum power output of 450hp. This beast can accelerate from 0-60mph in 5.6 seconds and have a top speed of 118 mph. It has a price of $67,070.

5. Lincoln Navigator

This vehicle is equipped with a 3.5-liter V6 engine that produces a maximum power output of 440 horsepower. This full-size SUV can fit 8 members in it and has a luxury experience. The top speed of this vehicle is 155 mph while the vehicle can accelerate from 0-60mph in 5.3 seconds. The price of this vehicle is $107,330.

6. Lincoln Blackwood

This was a special edition truck that was sold by Ford for just one year. This was equipped with a 5.4L V8 engine that can produce a maximum power output of 300 hp. The engine was paired to a 4-speed automatic transmission. It was priced at $58,800.

7. Ford Mustang GT500

It has a5.2L supercharged V8 engine that can generate a massive power output of  760 HP and 625 pound-feet of torque mated to rapid 7-speed dual-clutch transmission to the rear axle. It can hit 0 to 60 MPH in just 3.6 seconds and have a top speed of 180 mph. The starting price of the Ford Mustang GT500 is $77,260.

8. Ford Expedition limousine

This limousine can fit 12-16 passengers in it. Fully loaded with 2 TVs, VCR, and a party-starting audio system, the car is a luxurious machine. The interiors were decorated with fiber optic lighting and a star-gazer mirrored ceiling will take your breath away. 

9. 1969 Mach 1 Ford Mustang

This vehicle is the first generation of Mach 1 Ford Mustang. These were in production from 1969 to 1978 and had a 7.0 L Ram Air Cobra Jet & Super Cobra Jet V8 engine that would produce a maximum power of 335 hp at 5,200rpm and 440 lb-ft (597 N⋅m) of torque at 3,400rpm.

10. 1930 Lincoln Model L

These were in production from 1920 to 1930. This vehicle has a 6.3 L V8 engine that is capable of producing a maximum power output of 90hp and the engine is mated to a 3-speed manual transmission.
