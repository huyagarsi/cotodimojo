What is Al Dahra Company Qatar?

As per sources, Al Dahra is a Gulf-based company that provides services to the aerospace, security, and defense sectors. The aim of this company was to obtain high-tech Italian-made submarines that could evade radar detection.

The company was shut down in May 2023 after Qatar alleged that the company was spying for Israel and passing sensitive documents as per the latest reports. Well, in this case, the Qatar government has arrested eight employees of this company who are most probably Indian.

Why Al Dahra Global Technologies and Consultancy Services Holding Closed?

The company was closed in May 2023, and the reason behind its shutting down was that the company was spying for Israel and passing sensitive documents. Later the Qatar government allegedly arrested eight Indian employees of the company in August 2022 and sentenced them to death on October 26, 2023.
