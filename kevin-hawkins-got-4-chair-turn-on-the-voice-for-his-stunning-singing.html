Kevin Hawkins Is The Contestant From NBC The Voice

Kevin Hawkins is a Dallas-based singer and songwriter currently participating in The Voice season 22.

Hawkins made the headlines recently after appearing in season 22 of the singing reality show The Voice. He is admired as one of the most compelling contestants, with four chairs turned on the show.

He gave his extraordinary performance on the “Isn’t She Lovely” song in Blind auditions, capturing the attention of all four coaches. Among them, Kevin preferred to join Team Blake for his further journey on the singing reality show.

The Voice is one of the most famous and top-rating singing competitions, which has aimed to find talents of ages 13 or above since 2011. At the end of the show, the winner is determined through public voting.

Kevin Hawkins Is 28 Years Of Age

The 28 years old Lancaster native Kevin Hawkins celebrates his birthday every year on May 24th.

Kevin grew up in the Dallas area but later destined to Los Angeles in search of better opportunities in the entertainment field. Since he belongs to the musical family, he felt that music had been a great source of joy in his life.

He remembered the first day of school and said choosing music, unlike others, made him distinguishable from other students. After joining the University of North Texas, he began believing in himself more and realized he could accomplish anything in his life.

Kevin Hawkins Grew Up In A Musical Family

With the release of his debut single called “All Night Long,” Kevin Hawkins entered the music industry.

Born and raised in Lancaster, he realized his passion for singing from a young age. According to him, everybody in his family sings a little bit and plays musical instruments, including the piano, drums, the guitar.

Kevin’s family always inspired him to pursue a career in music; hence he referred to them as his support system.

Regarding his educational qualification, he graduated from the University of North Texas, where he completed a degree in Vocal Performance and Music Education. Unlike all students, he jumped right into the music.

The singer credits his University for making him more committed and confident. Also, modern-day Prince and John Legend are his biggest inspirations.

Moreover, he grew up in a musical family, but the details of his family were kept behind the wraps. His mother used to perform in the church choir while his father sang publicly for the first time in church at age 2.

Kevin “All Night Long” Song Was inspired By His Ex-girlfriend

Kevin’s “All Night Long” song was his first single released on October 7, 2019.

The song gained an immense appreciation and positive response from the audience, and the concept of this song came from his ex-partner. He was having an affair with someone whom he was supposed to marry but later got separated for some reason.

Hawkins portrays the feelings about spending the night with someone you love in his slow and sensual ballad. The R&B singer has been immersed in music all his life and is fully contented with what he has achieved throughout these years.
